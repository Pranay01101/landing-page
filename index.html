FAQs

What types of debt can Moneytor care help me with?

Most of your unsecured debts (not backed by a collateral) can be enrolled. If the majority of your debt load consists of personal loans, credit card debts, consumer loans, payday loans, peer-to-peer loans, BNPL loans, and education loans; you can expect a good result from our Debt Relief Platform.

What is Debt Settlement?

Debt settlement involves negotiating with creditors to reduce debt amounts or establish structured repayment plans, helping you regain control of your finances.

how does settlement affect my credit score?

settlement may harm your credit score since it involves paying less than the full debt, leading to a 'partial payment' notation on your credit report.

what is NPA (Non Performing Asset)?

NPA (Non Performing Asset) denotes loans or credits that are in default, resulting in no interest or principal payments for a specific duration.
